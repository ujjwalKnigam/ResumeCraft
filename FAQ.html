Frequently Asked Questions


What is a resume builder?

A resume builder is an online app or software that provides users with interactive forms and templates. The best applications of this type share insights and suggestions to help you give employers the right kind of information. For example, the Professional AutoResume resume builder features ready-made content tailored to the needs of specific job seekers. It makes it easier to word each section and communicate your value as an employee.

Is Professional AutoResume resume builder free?

Professional AutoResume resume builder is free to create a resume online. The Webpage follows the “try before you buy” philosophy and download it only after you are satisfied with the results. Downloading a printable resume as a PDF or .docx file from Professional AutoResume costs nothing.

What type of design should I choose for my resume?

First, do some research about standard resumes for your specific industry. Then, decide how you'll add your personal touch to that style. Consider which sections you'll want to feature, what order they should go in, and how you'll highlight the most important information.

Should I add a profile picture to my resume?

The answer will vary based on the industry or the job you are applying to. Some jobs would prefer a resume without a profile picture, while some might say that a profile picture helps. Take time to ask your peers about their resumes or reach out to the job you're applying to. You can always make multiple versions-one with and one without. And if you use a photo, make sure it's a high quality professional image.

How do I add multiple pages to my resume?

Design one page of your resume, then duplicate your project and use your first page as a template to create the second. Add as many pages to your resume as you'd like.
